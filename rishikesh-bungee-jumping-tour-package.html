Rishikesh Bungee Jumping Tour Package — Simple Guide

A friendly and easy-to-read guide for travellers who want to try bungee jumping in Rishikesh. This page explains what to expect, how to book, safety points, sample itineraries and useful tips — written in clear and simple English.

Why choose Rishikesh for Bungee Jumping?

Rishikesh is one of India's top adventure hubs. It sits by the Ganges river, surrounded by green hills. Here you will find one of the highest bungee jumping platforms in India. The jump site offers a clean, professional setup, trained staff and clear safety rules. Many travellers pick Rishikesh because the jump is paired with other activities like river rafting, short treks and yoga, so you can have a full fun day.

What is included in a typical bungee jumping package?

Most local packages are simple and include:

  • Return transfer from Rishikesh or a nearby pickup point
  • Visit to the jump site and safety briefing
  • One bungee jump with certified equipment and staff
  • Professional photos and short videos of your jump (usually extra or included depending on package)
  • Tea or light snacks after the jump

Some packages add extras like river rafting, lunch, or an overnight stay. Choose a package that matches how much time and comfort you want.

How to Book — Simple Steps

  1. Choose a trusted operator. Read reviews and check how long they have been running adventure activities.
  2. Check what the package includes and the pickup point. Decide if you want a private pick-up or a shared transfer.
  3. Book in advance, especially during weekends and holiday seasons. Adventure spots can fill up fast.
  4. On the booking day, reach the pickup point on time, carry your ID and wear comfortable clothes.

What to Expect on the Day

The bungee day is exciting and straightforward. Here is a simple flow of events:

  • Pickup & Transfer: You will be picked up and driven to the jump site.
  • Safety Briefing: Trained staff explain the jump, safety rules and demonstrate gear fitting.
  • Gear & Medical Check: Staff fit harnesses and check your health status briefly (you will be asked about any medical problems).
  • Jump: You climb the platform, get ready and jump under instruction. The staff monitor everything and guide you during the fall and rebound.
  • Photos & Video: Many operators offer photos and a short video clip of the jump as a memory.
  • Return: After the activity, you return to the pickup point or continue any add-on activities like rafting or lunch.

Safety — What You Need to Know

Safety is the top priority for all good operators. Simple safety notes:

  • Certified gear: Ropes, harnesses and platform are inspected and maintained. Ask your operator about their safety checks.
  • Trained staff: Well-trained staff guide you before, during and after the jump.
  • Medical check: You will be asked simple questions about heart problems, recent surgery, pregnancy or bone issues. People with these conditions are not allowed to jump.
  • Age and weight limits: Most operators set age and weight limits. Check these before booking.
  • Insurance: Adventure insurance can help, but verify with the operator what they provide and what you need to buy separately.

If you have doubts about your health, consult a doctor before booking. Never hide medical conditions — safety staff need to know.

Sample One-Day Itinerary (Bungee + Rafting Option)

This plan fits well for most travellers who start from Rishikesh town.

  • 08:00 AM: Pickup from Rishikesh hotel
  • 09:00 AM: Reach the jump site, complete paperwork and safety briefing
  • 10:00 AM: First jump (some people prefer to jump early to avoid nerves)
  • 11:00 AM: Photos, tea and short rest
  • 12:00 PM: Short drive to rafting start point (for those who booked rafting)
  • 01:00 PM: Rafting trip begins (2–3 hours for standard sections)
  • 04:00 PM: Lunch and return to Rishikesh

This schedule is flexible. Some travellers prefer rafting first and jump later. Choose the order that suits you.

Costs & What Affects Prices

Package prices vary by operator, season and inclusions. Typical price factors:

  • Transport type: Private pickup costs more than shared transfers.
  • Extras: Photos, videos, meal and rafting add extra cost.
  • Peak days: Weekends and holidays usually have higher demand and prices.

Ask for a clear price list before booking. Some operators allow partial payment online and remaining on the day.

Packing List — What to Carry

  • Comfortable sports clothing (no loose dresses or skirts)
  • Closed shoes or sports shoes — sandals are not ideal for harnessing
  • Valid photo ID (original)
  • Sunscreen, sunglasses and a hat for waiting time (remove before jump)
  • Small towel and a change of clothes if you plan rafting too
  • Cash for small expenses and tips

Tips to Make the Experience Better

  • Bring a calm friend: A friend can share photos and cheer you on.
  • Listen carefully: Staff instructions matter — follow them exactly.
  • Stay hydrated: Drink water before the activity but avoid heavy meals right before jumping.
  • Warm up: Some simple stretching helps your muscles before the jump.
  • Ask about photos: Decide if you want photos or videos included so there are no surprises.

Frequently Asked Questions (Quick Answers)

  • Is bungee safe? When run by trained staff with certified gear, bungee is safe for healthy people who meet the rules.
  • What if I'm scared? Fear is normal. Staff will support you and you can choose to step back any time before the jump.
  • How high is the jump? Heights vary by operator; Rishikesh offers high platforms that give a strong free-fall feeling.
  • Can children jump? Most operators set a minimum age and weight. Check operator rules.
