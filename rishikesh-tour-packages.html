Why Rishikesh Is a Perfect Travel Destination

Rishikesh is one of India’s most loved tourist destinations because it offers something for every mood and purpose. Here’s why travelers prefer visiting Rishikesh:

  • Spiritual Atmosphere: Rishikesh is home to many ancient temples, peaceful ashrams, and meditation centers.
  • Adventure Capital: It offers river rafting, bungee jumping, ziplining, cliff jumping, and more.
  • Natural Beauty: The river, mountains, forests, and waterfalls create a perfect scenic setup.
  • Easy Accessibility: Located near Haridwar and Dehradun, it is easy to reach by road, rail, and air.
  • Affordable Packages: Variety of budget, family, honeymoon, and adventure packages.

Best Time to Visit Rishikesh

The best time to visit Rishikesh is from October to June. Rafting is closed during the monsoon season, so adventure lovers should avoid July–September.

How to Reach Rishikesh

  • By Road: 5–6 Hours from Delhi
  • By Train: Rishikesh Station / Haridwar Junction
  • By Air: Jolly Grant Airport (20 KM)

Where to Stay in Rishikesh
